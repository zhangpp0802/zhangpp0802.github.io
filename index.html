Yiran Zhang

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. in Computer Science at Purdue University under the supervision of Dr. Aniket Bera. Before this, I was graduated at Northwestern University with a CS Master Degree and was fortunate to work with Dr. Emma Alexander, Dr. Diego Klabjan, and Dr. Ian Horswill. I also received my B.S. in Computer Science and B.A. in Visual Fine Art double degrees from Union College.

My research interest is in the field of computer graphics and VR/AR graphics and locomotion problems. I enjoy exploring various kinds of learning based methods to generate graphics models in VR. I also have a strong belief in the potential of VR/AR in our future daily use and thus I also work on projects that can help making this amazing technique come into our life soon.
